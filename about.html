Who We Are

QLD Group is the parent company of a network of transportation companies, managing and operating several subsidiaries to provide reliable, integrated transport and logistics solutions. Our Success Story Partners Since its establishment, QLD AB has consistently expanded its range of services to meet the diverse and growing needs of clients across both large and small sectors. The company has built a solid reputation in the Swedish market, partnering with renowned names such as DHL, BRING, DSV, POSTNORD, UPS, TNT, Fedex, MenigoGLC (Martin & servera),among others.

Qld AB History


- QLD AB was founded in Sweden on November 11, 2021, with a clear vision to become a leading partner in sustainable and reliable transport solutions for both businesses and individuals. The company launched with a strong commitment to providing exceptional transport services that meet high standards of quality and safety, with a focus on environmental sustainability Today, QLD AB proudly delivers comprehensive, eco-friendly transport services, reinforcing its role in environmental preservation and making it a trusted partner for clients across Sweden.


Our Vision:


To be the trusted and sustainable partner in transport solutions, supporting companies of all sizes and our local community.


Our Mission:


To provide high-quality transport services that meet the needs of all our clients, committed to the highest standards of safety and environmental sustainability while employing individuals of all abilities.
